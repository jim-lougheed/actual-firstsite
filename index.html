Fun Stuff

I'm a translator-turned-developer who has a passion for languages, both human and computer. I love to explore the ways through which we encode and decode the messages we convey to each other.

My Projects

KitchenCompanion

KitchenCompanion Customizable Cookbook is a React web application that assists users in finding countless recipe ideas based on ingredients on hand and helps them decide "What am I going to make for dinner tonight?"

The Simon Game

A challenge project from Angela Yu's Complete Web Development Bootcamp, this simple JavaScript game is simple to play. With each turn, a square will light up and add to a sequence. It is your job to remember the sequence and click the corresponding squares as it continues to get longer and longer.

Dino Game

This clone of the Chrome Dino game is based on a JavaScript tutorial from Web Dev Simplified but created using React. Press any key to start running and jump over the oncoming cacti!

My Skills

Web Development

Earlier this year, a couple good friends turned me onto the world of web development after I shared that I was looking for a new challenge in my career. Although my previous roles were challenging in their own ways, I wanted to gain skills that required me to create something with my own two hands. And having done a lot of my learning about languages and keyboards of other types, coding seemed like a natural way to flex my creativity. With each day, I learn more, build more and grow more curious about the endless applications of web development.

Language

I'm obsessed with the vast number of ways we can use sounds and symbols to convey complex ideas to one another, and often without even thinking about it. Nothing excites me more than examining new words for familiar concepts, new scripts containing familiar thoughts, or new slang to replace standard messages.

Baking

It's no secret I have a sweettooth, nor is it that it is spoiled with desserts and goodies. Due in part to the Great Canadian Baking Show, I have sank my teeth into the world of creating elegant and sugary desserts (and then enjoy sinking my teeth into the desserts themselves!). I've come a long way, but I have a lot to learn and bake before I earn the title of Star Baker.

Piano

Sitting down at the bench and transforming scribblings into melodies transports me to another world. Since I was a wee lad, I have been in love with the feeling of music emanating from my fingers. It's a beautiful thing.

Let's Chat
